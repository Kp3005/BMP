About PricePared.com

Who doesn’t like to save money on their purchases?

Comparison shopping is a habit that customers have developed that helps them find the best deals online. A price comparison app or website allows you to compare prices for products sold by various retailers. The price comparison tools then show you a list of retailers selling the same product, and how much they’re selling it for. Those looking to save money on purchases are more likely to use a price comparison tool to help them with their research. To attract budget-conscious customers you may need to offer a lower product price.
The customer can register themselves first through the sign up page followed by the OTP verification. Then the customer has to select the city and can select the items he/she wants to purchase and the software will compare the prices of that particular item available across the selected stores of a city. For example if a customer wants to purchase rice from Ahmedabad city then he will be shown the list of rice available across the stores in ahmedabad (like D-mart, Big-bazaar, JioMart etc.) in ascending order of its prices and each item will have “Quantity” and “Add to cart” buttons besides it. After this, the customers can add products to the cart (PLACE ORDER) and view the total bill amount in their cart. The customer will then receive a unique TOKEN (valid for 48 hrs) which they can show to the respective stores while they go for offline shopping. The store staff will then hand over the corresponding items to the customer directly by receiving the token ID. This will save time and money as well. The customer after the shopping will receive a FEEDBACK form to give their reviews regarding the store service.
